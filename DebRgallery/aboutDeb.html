My name is Deborah Ramey. I welcome you to a page that is just a little about me

I appreciate all of you that take the time to look and appreciate my work. I LOVE to paint! I have painted since I was very young. I was born and raised in Michigan until I was 13 and moved to Kentucky when my dad retired. Like in Michigan; I have won many art competitions here in Kentucky. I have sold many of my paintings here in Kentucky and Tennessee. I specialize in painting landscapes. I guess art is specific to all and landscapes give me a special canvas to create a world that I see in my head. I love peaceful places. Though this page is specific to my paintings; I also like to sing and code! I hope you enjoy my paintings ☺
